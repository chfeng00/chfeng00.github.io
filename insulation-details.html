Case: Basement Insulation Missing

  • House Age:: <15 Years
  • Comments:

    • Insulation of exterior wall in a finished basement is missing as shown in the IR photo. This implies the buyer has to pay larger hydro bill to maintain the room comfortable, but also possible mold growth in summer if the moisture barrier is incomplete or missing as well.

  • Recommendations:

    • Complete insulation and moisture barrier as soon as possible.
