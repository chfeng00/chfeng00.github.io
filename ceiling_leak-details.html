Case Study-Leaks(above the ceiling)

Case: Water Leaks behind Ceiling

  • House Age:: >50 Years
  • Comments:

    • Irregular high content of moisture was identified behind the second-floor bed room ceiling by using Infrared Imaging(IR) camera and confirmed by moisture meter. The moisture will not only damage the interior finishes, but also allow mold to grow.

  • Recommendations:

    • Call the qualified contractor to open the ceiling and further locate the leaky spots and source.

  • Cost:Depends on work needed
