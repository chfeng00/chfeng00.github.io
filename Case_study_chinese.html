    案例分析

    2. 案例分析

    Most of our inspection work is reporting unusual conditions- conditions that are present that should not be, and conditions that should be present but aren't. Therefore, the experience and knowledge of your home inspector is crucial for recognizing and reporting for anomalies. Although we are equipped with various of cutting-edge home inspection tools and gears, we only rely on them when we need to verify our visual observations. A good example is the use of thermal camera and moisture meter to trace the source of a leak. The results make sense only if you know when to use specific test equipment and the limitations of its use.
