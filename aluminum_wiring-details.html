Case Study-Aluminum Wiring

Case: Aluminum Wiring

  • House Age:: >50 Years
  • Comments:

    • This house was wired with aluminum. This was an accepted form of wiring. However, aluminum wiring can be an issue in regards to cost and availability of home insurance. Some companies may not insure a home with aluminum wiring unless it is inspected by a licensed electrical contractor or the Electrical Safety Authority (ESA), and a safety report is provided. Other companies may not offer insurance at all for homes with aluminum wiring and may require that it is replaced with copper wiring.

  • Recommendations:

    • It is important to check with your insurance carrier now, before signing to purchase the home, to ensure that you can obtain insurance.We recommend further evaluation from a licensed Electrician. More Information regarding aluminum wiring can be found at: https://www.esasafe.com/assets/files/esasafe/pdf/Flash_Notices/16-30-FL.pdf
